Privacy

How we handle your data

The short version. The profile text or CV you submit to the LinkedIn review tool is used only to generate your review, in the moment. We don't scrape LinkedIn, we don't sell your data, and we don't post anything on your behalf.

What we process. When you paste your profile or upload a CV and press review, that text is sent to our review service to produce a score and suggestions. If AI review is enabled, the text is sent to our AI provider solely to generate that result.

What we store. Account info you choose to create, and the roles/alerts you save, are stored in your browser for this demo. Review inputs are processed transiently to return your result and are not retained for marketing.

Your choices. Only submit your own profile. You can clear saved data anytime by signing out or clearing your browser storage. To request deletion of anything we hold, contact us.

Your rights (GDPR/CCPA). You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. We honor opt-outs of any non-essential processing.

This is a starter privacy notice for the EarlyRoles preview, not legal advice. Have it reviewed by counsel before public launch.
